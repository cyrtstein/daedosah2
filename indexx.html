Dae Do Sah is a Zen meditation group under the spiritual guidance of Zen Master Dae Gak of Furnace Mountain. We have weekly sitting groups in Rockville, Maryland and in Falls Church, Virginia. We also sponsor weekend retreats, one day retreats, and "Introduction to Meditation" Workshops several times a year. We've been around for just over two years now - we are still fairly small, and somewhat informal. Our "meditation halls" are really the living rooms of two of our founding members - Carol Kline and Curt Steinmetz.

Our Guiding Teacher, Zen Master Dae Gak, is a westerner who is both a Zen Master and a clinical psychologist. He is also the author of " Going Beyond Buddha", an excellent introduction to the practice of Zen. He has led retreats around the U.S., as well as in Europe, Australia and Korea.
Zen Master Dae Gak is one of 10 Zen Masters in the Kwan Um School of Zen.

Zen Master Dae Gak's teacher (our grand-teacher) is the well known Korean Zen Master Seung Sahn, the author of several popular books on Zen, including " Dropping Ashes on the Buddha". Zen Master Seung Sahn is the Founding Teacher of the Kwan Um School of Zen, and he is also very active in the Chogye Order of Korea (Korea's main Buddhist denomination).
